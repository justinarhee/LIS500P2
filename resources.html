Understanding Implicit Bias

What is Implicit Bias

  • The unconscious attitudes or stereotypes that affect our understanding, actions, and decisions.
  • Implicit bias operates subconciously

What is Intersectionality

  • Intersectionality is a framework that helps us understand how different aspects of a person's idenity can overlap and create different experiences of discrimination.
  • The term was created by a scholar to describe how Black Women experience discrimination differently from Black Men and White Men and Women

What is IBT

  • The Implicit Bias Test (IBT) is a tool that is used to measure unconcious bias that people may hold towards different social groups.
  • It was developed by a Harvard University Project
  • Implicit Bias can afect our judgements and the IBT helps us uncover these biases
