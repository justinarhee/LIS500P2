Kimberly Bryant

Early Life

  • Born on January 14, 1967 in Memphis, Tennessee
  • Excelled at math and science, earned a scholarship to Vanderbuilt University
  • Majored in Electrical engineering and minored in Computer Science and Math

Black Girls Code

  • Founded Black Girls Code in 2011 to introduce young girls to STEM Fields
  • The organization focuses on Computer Science, Robotics, AI, and Technology
  • Aims to increase diversity in the tech industry
  • Has reached thousands of girls across the United States

Awards and Recogintion

  • Was named one of the top 25 most influential African Americans in Tech
  • Recognized by the White House as a champion of change
  • Featured on Forbes, CNN, and The New York Times for her work in tech
